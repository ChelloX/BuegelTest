Reachability Graph Statusbar

Statusbar States

The statusbar shows information on the actual reachability graph. In the first image you see the standard statusbar. It contains a legend and a counter for vertices and edges in the graph. The legend represents the place id's of the petri net in the order as they are in the brackets of the markings.

If you click on the double-arrow symbol the legend will replace the id's of the places with it's names. Sure, only if the name of a place is set.

Sometimes an orange exclamation mark is additionally shown in the statusbar. That means, that the reachability graph wants your attention. Hover over the exclamation mark to get a tooltip message with further information.
