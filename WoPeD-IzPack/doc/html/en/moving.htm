Moving elements

To move a net element, just left-click to it, drag it, and drop it at the new position.

  Note that you need to click just a few pixels off the center of the symbol in order to move it (that is where the cursor is shown in a crossed arrow shape ).

If the cursor gets a pointing finger shape, then the arc drawing mode is enabled and moving the element will not work.

Moving tags

Tags are either trigger symbols, resource assignments or names of net elements. To move a tag independently from the net element itself to another position, left click on the net element and then - after a short delay in order to avoid interpretation as a double-click - a second time on the tag you wish to move. When you see a small dashed box around the tag, it is ready to be moved to another position just by drag-and-drop using the left-mouse. Note that it is recommended to choose a location for a tag in the vicinity of the associated net element in order to guarantee a clear understanding of the Petri net semantics.
