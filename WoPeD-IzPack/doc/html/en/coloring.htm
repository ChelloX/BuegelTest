Handle Coloring

For better unserstandability WoPeD is able to colorize the handles of a net. You can activate the coloring by pressing the "" button in the analyze menu and disable it by pressing again.

 

 

You can change the properties in the configuration menu under "Coloring".

 

 

If you want to change the used colors just click on one of the colors and the color-chooser will appear. You can reset the colors by pressing the reset-button.
