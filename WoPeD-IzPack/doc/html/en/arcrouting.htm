Arc routing

Usually, arcs are drawn as shortest lines between the two connected elements. It is also possible to specify routing elements in order to "bend" the arc line around one or more intermediate points:

  • Move the mouse pointer to the arc to be routed
  • Right-click to open the context menu
  • Choose  " Add point"
  • A small square (the arc handle) appears on the line
  • Drag the arc handle to the desired position

The arc line can either be drawn to form a rounded curve or straight along the arc points as corners. The default behaviour (which must refer to the whole Petri net) can be set within the configuration dialog. To remove a routing point, move the mouse to its location and choose  " Remove point" from the context menu.
