Sidebar


The sidebar is a switchable element on the right side of the editor window.
It contains two things: the overview and the treeview.

The overview is a small overview of the editor and its content. When editing a large net you can use it to navigate within your net.

The treeview contains all the elements of the current net. You can select an element by clicking on its name in the treeview. In order to select multiple elements hold down your Ctrl key and click on the elements you want to have in your selection. The overviewpanel and the treeview panels can be closed by clicking the red close button or by unchecking the corresponding checkbox in the view-ribbon.

To change the horizontal size of the sidebar you can grab its right border and drag it until fits your needs. You can also change the size of the overview and treeview panels by grabbing the border in the middle.



Status and size of the overview and treeview panel are beeing saved with each petrinet and will be restored when loading the net.
