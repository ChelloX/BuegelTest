BPEL Activity "wait"

The wait activity stops process execution for a specified amount of time or until a deadline is reached.

 

The wait activity tells the business process to wait for a given time period or until a certain time has passed.

 

To add a wait activity to the process manually:

1. In the Properties view, select a Wait Type: Duration or Deadline.

2. In the Expression box, specify one of the following:

For a duration, specify a time

 

For a deadline, specify a date and time
