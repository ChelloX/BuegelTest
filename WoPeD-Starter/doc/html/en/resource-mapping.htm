Resource mapping

In many cases the execution of a workflow task (described by a transition of a Petri net) depends on the availability of specialized resource objects having a certain skill or belonging to a certain organizational unit. In the example process fragment below, work items resulting from task t4 have to wait until a secretary (= a role) from the New York location (= a group) is available.

WoPeD allows the assignment of exactly one role and one group to each resource-triggered transition in order to express this kind of dependency. This so-called resource mapping can be defined within the transition property dialog.
