Coverability Graph Statusbar

Statusbar States

The statusbar shows information on the actual coverability graph. In the first image you see the standard statusbar. It contains a legend and a counter for vertices and edges in the graph. The legend represents the place ids of the petri net in the order they are in the brackets of the markings.

If you click on the double-arrow symbol, the legend will replace the ids of the places with their names (if the name of a place is set).

Sometimes an orange exclamation mark is additionally shown in the statusbar. That means, that the coverability graph asks for one's attention. Hover over the exclamation mark in order to get a tooltip message with further information.
