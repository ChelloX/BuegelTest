Triggers

Triggers are classifications of the external behaviour of transitions and part of the extended syntax of workflow nets. There are four available kinds of triggers:

  • Message trigger
    The occurrence of the transition depends on the arrival of a message from outside of the the modelled business process
  • Resource trigger
    The occurrence of the transition depends on the availability of a resource object, e.g. a human workflow participant
  • Time trigger
    The occurrence of the transition depends on a certain point of time which has to be reached.
  • No trigger
    The occurrence of the transition depends on nothing but its usual preconditions (= tokens on input places) and can be executed automatically
